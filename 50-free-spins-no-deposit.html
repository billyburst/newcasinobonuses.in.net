Tips for Getting the Most From Your Free Spins

Once you've claimed your 50 free spins, there are a few things you can do to make the most of them. Finally, remember that the size of the bonus doesn't always reflect its quality. A 50 free spins offer with no wagering and a £100 win cap could be better value than 100 free spins with 40x wagering and a £20 cap. Always look at the full picture before deciding which offer to go for.
